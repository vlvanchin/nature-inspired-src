# Bottle Gourd, Ivy Gourd and Indian Goose-Berry Juice

## Preparation

* a 100gm size of bottle gourd

* 5nos of ivy gourd

* 1 Indian goose berry

## Method

* Just put them in a mixer grinder to extract the juice.

* Now ready to drink.
